Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. What We Collect

ZAR collects minimal data to operate the game:

  • Display name: The name you enter each session, held only for the duration of that game session and deleted when the room closes.
  • Gameplay data: Card plays, scores, and game actions, retained in memory only and not persisted beyond the session (unless optional Redis persistence is enabled, in which case it is deleted within 24 hours).
  • IP address / connection metadata: Automatically collected by our hosting provider for security and abuse-prevention purposes.

2. What We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect email addresses, phone numbers, or any contact information.
  • We do not use tracking cookies or advertising trackers.
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Voice Chat

If you use the optional voice chat feature, audio is transmitted peer-to-peer via WebRTC. We do not record or store voice audio.

4. Error Tracking

We may use Sentry to collect anonymised error reports to improve stability. These reports include technical details (stack traces, browser/OS info) but do not include your display name or game content.

5. Data Retention

Session data (names, scores) is deleted automatically when your game room closes or within 24 hours at most. We do not maintain long-term databases of user activity.

6. Your Rights

Because we do not store personally identifiable information beyond your current session, there is nothing to access, correct, or delete after your session ends. If you have concerns, contact us.

7. Children

ZAR is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Changes

We may update this policy. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance.

9. Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us at the contact information on our website.
